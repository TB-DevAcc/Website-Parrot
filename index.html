Don't Pay. Just Speak.

This is the language Meetup you've been waiting for.

Organized.

The events are organized by a community buddy. If nobody volunteers a donation pot is opened and whoever is interested in organizing an event can claim it. Donations are of course voluntary.

Goal-oriented.

We know, that you've come here to learn a language. Drinking a cold beer and meeting new interesting people can be a pleasant side-effect, but shouldn't be the focus. Our events have a itinerary and a structure that makes it easy for everybody to try themselves in a their new language.

Fun.

Sticking to a boring language course can be tough. In the end we're all just dreaming of going out there to communicate. So let's just do that and have fun!

Get started in your city.

We collect donations and help you organize a language event in your city. You set a number and as soon as we gathered the funding, the event can start up!

Learn from natives.

What's the best way to get up and running with your new language? Listen to people, that use it everyday. This ensures you're not learning boring vocab lists you'll never use, but instead focus on what's important in every day life.
